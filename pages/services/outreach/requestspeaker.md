OSC is dedicated to enhancing understanding of the laws that our office enforces through comprehensive training. Browse our training catalog below to discover the perfect program for your agency's training session or event and email us at [**certification@osc.gov**](mailto:certification@osc.gov) to reserve your spot. When emailing [**certification@osc.gov**](mailto:certification@osc.gov), you can include the details of your training session or event in the body of your email or utilize the speaker request form provided below. Separate forms are available for requests from federal agencies and for requests from non-federal entities.​​

**Training Catalog** 

**Navigating Whistleblower Complaints: Effective Response Strategies**. (60 minutes). Unlock essential skills with our annual supervisory training! As mandated by the Dr. Chris Kirkpatrick Whistleblower Protection Act, this training equips you with vital tools to handle disclosures of potential government wrongdoing and allegations of retaliation. Dive into practical tips, real-world scenarios, and interactive exercises designed to enhance your management capabilities and navigate these challenging situations effectively. Make this training your go-to resource for mastering whistleblower protection and response.

**Safeguarding the Merit System: A Guide to Preventing Prohibited Personnel Practices.** (90 minutes). Master the 14 PPPs with OSC's comprehensive training! Explore critical areas like unlawful hiring practices and whistleblower retaliation through insightful discussions led by a top attorney. Gain a deeper understanding with real-life case studies and nuanced legal explanations. Whether you're a manager, non-manager, HR specialist, or agency counsel, this training is designed to meet your needs. This training satisfies one of the requirements of OSC's 2302(c) Certification Program but can also be provided outside of the certification process.

**Hatch Act Training.** (60 minutes). Join OSC for an informative session where an expert attorney will break down the Hatch Act's restrictions on partisan political activities for government employees. Discover what political activities are allowed, explore recent OSC case examples, and gain a clear understanding of OSC's roles, including investigating potential violations and issuing advisory opinions. Don't miss this opportunity to get up to speed on crucial compliance issues!​

**Understanding USERRA: Protecting the Employment Rights of Service Members.** (60 minutes). Join us for a comprehensive training on the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA), designed to equip federal supervisors and employees with essential knowledge about the rights and responsibilities related to military service. This session will cover the key provisions of USERRA, including the protection of job rights for returning service members, the prevention of discrimination based on military status, and the obligations of employers to provide reinstatement and benefits. By the end of this training, attendees will have a clearer understanding of how to navigate the complexities of USERRA. 

**Tailored Training. ​**(length varies). OSC can develop tailored trainings based on agency need. You can email [**certification@osc.gov**](mailto:certification@osc.gov) to discuss developing a tailored training for your agency.​
