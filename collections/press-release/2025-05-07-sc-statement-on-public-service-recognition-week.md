WASHINGTON, D.C./May 7, 2025 – In honor of Public Service Recognition Week, I would like to take this opportunity to thank every member of the U.S. Office of Special Counsel (OSC) team, and the federal, state, county, and local employees across the country, for their dedication and commitment to serving our nation.  

“As designee for Acting Special Counsel Jamieson Greer, I am proud to recognize the hard work of my OSC colleagues who work on behalf of the federal employees who turn to our agency to disclose waste, fraud, and abuse,” said Senior Counsel Charles Baldis. From investigating alleged prohibited personnel practices to providing Hatch Act Advisory opinions on the political activities of employees, or receiving disclosures from whistleblowers about government wrongdoing, OSC’s dedicated team works hard on behalf of American citizens and make sure that taxpayers get their money’s worth.  

As we celebrate Public Service Recognition Week, I would like to thank the OSC team, as well as the dedicated federal employees who come to OSC for assistance. It is an honor to be part of the federal workforce with so many talented and devoted public servants.
